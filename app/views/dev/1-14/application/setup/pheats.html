Are you applying as part of the plant health exports audited trader scheme (PHEATS)?

You're enrolled in PHEATS. You can choose whether to apply under the scheme or not. If you choose not to apply under PHEATS, you'll need to book an inspection to get an export health certificate. The charges may be different depending on how you choose to apply. Find out more about PHEATS.
