Check whether you can apply for re-issue

You can ask for a certificate to be re-issued if the original was lost or damaged. You may also get a re-issue if the certificate has expired or if the details printed on it are wrong.

You will not be able to change all of the details on a re-issued certificate. For example, you cannot change the destination country or what's in your consignment.

You will pay the normal fee (opens in new tab) for a phytosanitary certificate. If you're asking for a re-issue because APHA made a mistake, you should not be charged.
