If you are sending fruit, vegetables and cut flowers to the European Union or Northern Ireland then you could be eligible to join the plant health exports audited trader scheme (PHEATS). The scheme trains you to do your own inspections, which can help you get export certificates more quickly.

It can take several months to register and complete training. You can find out more about PHEATS on GOV.UK.
