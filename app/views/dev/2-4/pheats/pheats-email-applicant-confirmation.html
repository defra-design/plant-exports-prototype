What happens next

We’ll email the responsible person and authorised people to ask them to agree to the terms and conditions. It’s important that they respond to this email. Your application will not move forward until they have all agreed to the terms and conditions.

We will then arrange for authorised people to start training and for a site visit.

How to speed up your application

You can help the application process go quickly by encouraging the responsible person and authorised people to respond to the email asking them to agree to the terms and conditions.

It’s also a good idea to get your inspection station and holding point set up properly. If these areas aren’t set up properly, this can delay your application.
