What happens next

  1. We'll send a confirmation email to your email address. The email will contain a link to the terms and conditions.
  2. When everyone involved in PHEATS has accepted the terms and conditions, the inspector will arrange your training.
  3. The inspector will also do an initial site inspection. This usually takes around 2 hours.
  4. Once you've completed these steps, your organisation will be fully registered for PHEATS. You'll then be able to start doing your own inspections.
