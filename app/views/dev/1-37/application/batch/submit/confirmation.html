Your APHA sample references are listed below
! Warning Your application may be rejected if you do not follow these instructions.

What you need to do

  1. Label your samples using the APHA sample references.
  2. Send your labelled samples to the address on this page. You must send in any relevant copies of your import permits with your samples.
  3. Check your application status has changed to ‘report issued’. This usually happens within 7 days.
  4. You can then apply for phytosanitary certificates for commodities that pass testing.
  5. You can apply for more phytosanitary certificates using the same sample reference details until the commodity in your possession is used up.

We'll only contact you if there is a problem with your application.
