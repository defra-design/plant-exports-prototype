What happens next

We prioritise certificate re-issues over other types of application.

  1. We will check and process your application for re-issue.
  2. If there are no problems, we will re-issue your certificate.
  3. If you have the original certificate, return it to your local inspector or to this address:

    CIT Plants Headquarters
    APHA
    Foss House
    Kings Pool
    1-2 Peasholme Green
    York
    YO1 7PX

We'll only contact you if there is a problem with your application.

You will pay a lower fee (opens in new tab) to get a phytosanitary certificate re-issued. If you need a re-issue because APHA made an error, you will not be charged.
